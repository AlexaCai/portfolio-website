My name is Alexandre, and I am a passionate full-stack web developer located in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. I have 3 years of experience working with data and technologies, and about 1 year working on web development projects more specifically.

About me

Who are you Alex? What’s your background? How did you get into the web development universe?

Put simply, I am a former graduate professional who worked mostly on projects closely combining urban planning and data-related decisions/ practices/considerations.

Having worked on data driven decision-making projects for municipal investments as a professional researcher, and having supported different organizations on their technological projects deployments coming from a smart cities initiative as a Senior analyst in data and tech policies, I’ve decided in early 2023 to take a leap into web programming to consolidate my skills on a more technical level.

More concretely, my goal was to develop the necessary knowledge to be able to build useful web apps, websites and mobile apps that could meet the many challenges and organizations needs I observed on the field over the years.

From Professional researcher to Senior analyst in data and technology policies to Web developer? Really?

Yes, really. This career evolution has allowed me to acquire a lot of knowledge and skills in the field of data and technology, both at a high level and at a more operational level. And the best part of this journey is that I am currently thrilled about where I am, and passionate about my current field and the challenges that await me in web development.

I started to enjoy this domain as soon as I learned how to write my first codes. I knew I was on the right track. I always wanted to learn more and build more projects. And that's still the case today, especially knowing that I can count on a base of very useful complementary professional experience that will continue to help me in all my future web development work.
