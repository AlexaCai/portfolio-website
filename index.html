My name is Alexandre, and I am a passionate full-stack web developer located in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. I have 3 years of experience working with data and technologies, and about 1 year working on web development projects more specifically.

About me

So who are you Alex? What’s your background? How did you get into the web development universe?

Put simply, I am a former graduate professional who worked mostly on projects closely combining urban planning and data-related decisions.

Having worked on data-driven decision making projects for municipal investments as a professional researcher, and having supported different organizations on their technological project deployments coming from a governmental Smart Cities Challenge as a Senior analyst in data and technology policies, I’ve decided in early 2023 to take a leap into web programming to consolidate my skills on a more technical level.

More concretely, my goal was to develop the necessary knowledge to be able to build useful websites, web apps and mobile apps that could meet the many challenges and organizational needs I observed in the field over the years.

From Professional researcher to Senior analyst in data and technology policies to Web developer? Really?

Yes, really. This professional path allowed me to acquire great knowledge and skills in the field of data and technology, both at a high level and at a more operational level. And the best part of this professional journey is that I am currently thrilled about where I am now, and passionate about web development and the projects that await me in this domain.

I started to enjoy web development as soon as I learned how to write my first codes. I knew I was on the right track. I always wanted to learn more and build more projects. And that's still the case today, especially considering that I can count on a base of very useful complementary professional experiences that will continue to help me in my future web development works.

What are your interests in this field? What makes you want to do this every day?

I’d say the thing I like the most about web development is the fact that the learning possibilities are endless. I am an avid learner, as my previous studies in business, anthropology, archeology, languages, urban planning and project management proves it.

I realized very early in my journey to become a web developer that almost all of the time, the only limits on what you can possibly create for a digital product are (1) your own creativity and (2) your skills to operationate your ideas. Both of these two aspects need continuous stimulation and training, and that's why I am continuously aiming at improving my coding skills day after day. I am also aiming at enhancing my knowledge on complementary topics such as UX design, UI design, digital marketing and data analytics both by strong personal interest and professional value.

More technically speaking, I have a particular interest in building frontend products, responsive design and appealing visuals. I particularly enjoy working with React, React Native and React Bootstrap, as well as with manipulating databases in MongoDB or PostgreSQL. That being said, all technologies and tools truly remain of interest to me, including everything related to backend.
